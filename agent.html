Data connection

Connect your Google Sheet

Your sheet is the database. Agents read from it, write to it, and keep it updated.

1

Create your sheet

Make a Google Sheet with columns like Name, Email, Phone, Message. Each row = one action.

2

Paste Sheet URL

Copy your sheet's URL and paste it into AgentFlow. We'll connect it via OAuth2 — completely secure.

3

Map your columns

Tell the agent which column is the email, which is the name, etc. Point and click — no code needed.

4

Agent reads & acts

The agent loops through each row and does its job — sends the email, message, or logs the response.
